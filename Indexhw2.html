About Me

I've been interested in web design for a very long time. My initial education was in photography and film. I attended the Minneapolis College of Art and Design before completing a BA in Communications at the U of M Twin Cities. I've always had a facination with composition, negative and positive space and use of words and symbols.

I am currently taking a web development bootcamp at the U of M Twin Cities in the hopes of becoming a web developer in the near future.
